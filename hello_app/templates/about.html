About Weather History Tracker

The Weather History Tracker is a free web application designed to provide users with easy access to historical weather data based on:

  • Rainfall
  • Temperature
  • Wind Speed
  • Sunrise and sunset

Data is taken from https://open-meteo.com/en/docs/historical-forecast-api, a free source of weather data.
